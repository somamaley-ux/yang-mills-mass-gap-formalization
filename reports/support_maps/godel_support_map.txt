Current honesty boundary

What is now encoded honestly:

- a strengthened same-domain theorem surface for the paper's main theorem chain
- the opening inferential-life and closure-transmission bridge lemmas
- coding-layer, readback, and negative-fixed-point witness objects
- concrete syntax/substitution/readback objects and witness synthesis
- a concrete gate-collapsed contradiction layer for synthesized fixed points
- a concrete failed-branch inertness layer for repair/generation/downstream restoration
- a trajectory-linked failed-branch layer using existing `System`/`Redescription` primitives
- prerequisite packaging for same-domain Godel threats
- code-predicate role classification and gate-collapse packaging
- semantic, infinitary, meta-foundational, and extension rescue-route classification
- alignment and translation-filter compression theorems
- an explicit imported appendix-witness ledger for reused repo theorems
- a label-level appendix import ledger for the exact prose-cited theorem labels
- the terminal route-exhaustion theorem and main non-instantiability theorem
- the interior-stability corollary surface

What is not yet encoded as full local mathematical machinery:

- richer internal syntax algebra beyond the current token-level `SyntaxToken` layer
- deeper typed content-carrier semantics proving lawful equivalence from lower-level content rules
- the full standing-quotient and trajectory package cited in prose as theorem-by-theorem imported witnesses

Bottom line
-----------

The current Lean support provides an honest strengthened theorem surface for the
paper's same-domain route-exhaustion and non-instantiability claims.

It should still be described as a lightweight-to-intermediate paper-facing
formalization, not as a full mechanization of the manuscript's coding,
diagonal, and semantic infrastructure.
