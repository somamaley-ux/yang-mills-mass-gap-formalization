\begin{abstract}
This packet executes Phase~IX of the clay-closure upgrade plan on top of the hardened proof surface produced by Phases~I--VIII. It assembles, in one synchronized audit document, the final pass conditions, the load-bearing theorem-node closure matrix, the submission-package inventory, the final sign-off checklist, and the blocker-by-blocker closure memo. The packet adds no mathematics and is not a proof home. Its purpose is narrower: it makes explicit the internal package-level decision rule that must be satisfied before the manuscript suite is handed to outside referees and specialist auditors.
\end{abstract}
