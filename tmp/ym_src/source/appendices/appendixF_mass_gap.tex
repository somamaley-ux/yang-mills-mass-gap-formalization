\subsection*{Route~1 reading guide (where the closure is organized)}
This appendix is written so that the \emph{single load-bearing Route~1 entrance mechanism} can be audited without hunting across lanes.
A concentrated first reading typically focuses on five points: (i) the Plan-A $\chi^2$ gate and its explicit feasibility budget,
(ii) the same-scale Wilson-to-patched transport theorem (Theorem~\ref{thm:Wilson_patch_transport}) used to return the chart-truncated Lane~C estimates to the physical Wilson theory before the one-shot blocked transfer,

(iv) the hardening from entrance-scale blocked decay to a full fixed-lattice OS gap theorem, and
(v) the conversion of that lower bound into a continuum spectral gap.
